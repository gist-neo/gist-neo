# Source and image notes

- Public page structure and text were organized from the existing NEO Lab Google Sites pages.
- Google Sites serves many images through temporary/protected `sitesv-images-rt` URLs. Those files returned access errors during automated collection, so they are not copied into this package.
- Placeholder SVGs are original assets created for this starter and can be safely replaced with your own photographs.
- Do not publish third-party portraits or campus photos without confirming reuse rights.
